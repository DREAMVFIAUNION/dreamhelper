# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment
DREAMVFIA UNION is committed to building a respectful, professional, and constructive environment for contributors, collaborators, and community members.

## Expected behavior
- Be respectful and professional.
- Give constructive feedback.
- Assume good intent while staying clear and direct.
- Focus on the work, not personal attacks.
- Respect different backgrounds, experiences, and viewpoints.

## Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment, hate speech, or discrimination
- Personal attacks or intimidation
- Trolling, inflammatory behavior, or bad-faith disruption
- Sharing private or sensitive information without permission
- Repeatedly derailing technical or community discussions

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may remove comments, lock discussions, close issues, or restrict participation when behavior violates this code of conduct.

## Reporting
If you encounter unacceptable behavior, report it through the DREAMVFIA UNION GitHub organization or repository maintainers.
